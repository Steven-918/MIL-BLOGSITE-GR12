Activity 8

Write your initial reaction or response to the given situation. Place your output on your MIL blog.

1. Somebody posted something that precisely differs from your opinion about government officials.

- "Oh, interesting! It seems we've got a difference of opinions on government officials. That's the beauty of diverse perspectives, right? Let's keep the conversation respectful and maybe find some common ground."

2. Your name was mentioned in a comment that annoys another individual.

- "Yikes! My name in an annoying comment? That's a bit unexpected. Wonder what's going on there. Let's hope things settle down or maybe I can jump in and clear things up."

3. Your best friend is experiencing “Cyber Bullying”.

- "Cyberbullying hitting close to home with my best friend? Not cool. Time to step in, offer some support, and maybe report those trolls. No one messes with my buddies!"

4. Someone had posted a picture of you without asking permission.

- "A picture without permission? Seriously? Well, that's a bit of a breach. Let's address that calmly and maybe have a chat about the importance of asking before sharing."

5. Two of your friends are arguing on something using Facebook.

- "Friends arguing on Facebook? Drama alert! I might try to ease the tension, throw in a light joke, or encourage them to take it offline. We all know how these online arguments can get out of hand."
